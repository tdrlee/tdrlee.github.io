About Me

Research Interests

My research focuses on advancing Image and Video Generation using Diffusion Models in the field of core AI, while actively exploring ways to translate these methods into medical imaging applications. I am particularly interested in applying these technologies to MRI, CT, and X-ray data, with research directions including 3D reconstruction and unsupervised anomaly detection. My ultimate goal is to develop generalizable AI models that not only push the boundaries of generative modeling but also contribute to practical improvements in medical diagnostics.
